How does Lume detect fires? ▼
Lume uses YOLOv8, a state-of-the-art deep learning model, combined with OpenCV for video processing. Our system analyzes video frames in real-time, detecting both smoke and fire patterns with high accuracy. We've fine-tuned our model using advanced data augmentation techniques to minimize false detections.
What happens when a fire is detected? ▼
When Lume detects a fire for 5 consecutive seconds across multiple video frames, it automatically alerts emergency services through our Twilio integration. The system includes a user confirmation feature - if you decline an alert but fire is still detected, Lume will continue monitoring and prompting to ensure no potential threats are missed.
